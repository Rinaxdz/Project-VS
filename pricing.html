💡 No surprises, ever

We confirm travel costs before finalising any booking. Every quote includes an itemised travel line so you always know exactly what you're paying.

🏙️ Areas We Serve

Toronto Mississauga Brampton Vaughan Markham Richmond Hill Scarborough Etobicoke North York Pickering Ajax Whitby Oshawa Burlington Oakville Milton Newmarket Aurora Barrie Hamilton + surrounding areas

📏 How distance is calculated

Distance is measured from our base to your event venue by the most direct route. Confirmed on your consultation call before anything is finalised.

⚠️ Events over 60 km

Events beyond 60 km may carry an additional setup fee. We'll always walk you through the full cost before you commit — no obligation.

🚗 Outside the GTA?

We're open to travelling further for the right event. Reach out and we'll let you know if we can make it work — we love a new adventure.
