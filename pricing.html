Travel Policy

We come to you —
across the GTA

We proudly serve families across the Greater Toronto Area. Here's exactly how our travel fees work — no surprises.

📍

GTA Travel Zones

Free Zone
First 20 km from our base
Free No charge
Standard Travel
20 km – 60 km
$0.70/km Per km after 20 km
Extended Travel
Over 60 km
$0.70/km + Setup fee may apply

📏 How we calculate distance

Distance is measured from our base location to your event venue using the most direct route. We'll confirm the exact travel charge during your consultation call before any booking is finalized.

🏙️ Areas we serve

We proudly serve Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Scarborough, Etobicoke, North York, Pickering, and surrounding GTA communities.

⚠️ Events over 60 km

For events more than 60 km away, an additional setup fee may be required. This will be discussed transparently during your consultation — we'll always give you the full picture before you commit.

💡 No travel surprises

We never charge travel fees without telling you first. Every quote we send includes an itemized travel cost so you always know exactly what you're paying for.
